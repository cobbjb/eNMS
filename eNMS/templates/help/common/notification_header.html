The notification header supplies any extra information to the notification content. This appears in the notification body starting on the "Header:" line.

Example 1 - Regular text

Any normal text put in this field will show up in the notification after the "Header:" line.


Sample output:

  Service: Your Service
  Runtime: 2021-04-29 21:03:19.745496
  Status: PASS
  Results: ...
  Header: Report details will follow

Example 2 - Variable substitution

Similar to example 1, but now you can use Variable substitution.

The replaced text will show up in the notification after the "Header:" line. Note that some formatting may be necessary - and our example will use the dict_to_string function (see Variables - scroll down to find dict_to_string).


Sample output:

  Service: Your Service
  Runtime: 2021-04-29 21:03:19.745496
  Status: PASS
  Results: ...
  Header:
       data:
                key1: value1
                key2:
                        - 16
                        - 1024

       more_data:
                key1: value2
